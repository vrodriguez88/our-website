What is the SAT?

The SAT is a general test of verbal and quantitative reasoning accepted for college admissions in the US. The test is required for admission to undergraduate programs for most US colleges and universities.

How long is the SAT?

The testing session is 3 hours and 50 minutes long.

How much does the SAT cost?

The test is $54.50 with the essay and $43 without the essay.

What are the different sections of the SAT?

The sections of the SAT are math, evidence-based reading and writing. An optional essay is also offered.
