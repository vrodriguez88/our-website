What is the ACT?

The SAT is a standardized test admissions officers use to compare applicants from different schools and different states.

How long is the ACT?

The testing session is 2 hours and 55 minutes long. The testing session is 3 hours and 35 minutes with optional Writing Portion.

How much does the ACT cost?

The test is $34 to $49.50.

What are the different sections of the ACT?

The sections of the ACT are english, math, reading and science. An optional writing portion is also offered.
