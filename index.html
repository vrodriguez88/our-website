How to Apply to College 101

  1. Take your SATS
  2. Realize that you could do better and sign up to take it again
  3. Retake your SATS
  4. Wonder if you should be taking the ACTS
  5. Go on some college tours
  6. Get free stuff
  7. Shop at college store even though you know you're not going there
  8. File your FAFSA
  9. Worry about not getting into your top school
  10. Save up money for applications! (they can be exspensive!)
  11. Hear back from some schools!
  12. Recieve your Financial Aid Packages
  13. Tour your favorite schools again
  14. Decide on your school of choice!
  15. Yay! You are going to college woooohooo!
  16. Celebrate! (you totally deserve it!)
